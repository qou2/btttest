Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bedrock Tier Testing ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Important Note

Bedrock Tier Testing is designed with a minimal data collection philosophy. We do not require user accounts, do not store IP addresses, and only collect the specific information necessary for tier testing functionality.

Information We Collect

Player Information

  • Minecraft username
  • Rank/tier information related to gameplay
  • Region information (to help match players)

This information is used solely for the purpose of displaying tier rankings and facilitating gameplay matchups.

Gameplay Data

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, etc.)
  • Gameplay performance metrics
  • Match history

This information is used to calculate tier rankings and provide accurate player statistics.

Information We Do Not Collect

Excluded Data

  • IP addresses
  • Email addresses
  • Payment information
  • Personally identifiable information beyond your Minecraft username
  • Cookies or tracking information
  • Location data beyond your selected region

How We Use Information

Tier Rankings

  • Calculate and display tier rankings based on performance
  • Match players with others of similar skill level
  • Display leaderboards and statistics

Service Improvement

  • Analyze aggregated gameplay data to improve game mechanics
  • Optimize matchmaking systems
  • Identify and address issues or bugs

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties. Your gameplay information is visible to other players only in the context of leaderboards, match history, and public gameplay statistics.

Public Information

  • Your Minecraft username
  • Your current tier/rank
  • Your region
  • Public match statistics

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited information we collect. Since we do not collect sensitive personal information, our security focus is on maintaining the integrity and availability of gameplay data.

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences, including players under 13 years old. Since we collect only Minecraft username, tier/rank, and region information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 that would require parental consent under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Discord Integration

If you choose to join our Discord server, please note that Discord has its own Privacy Policy governing the information collected through their service. We recommend reviewing Discord's Privacy Policy if you have concerns about information shared through Discord.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Data Retention

We retain gameplay data for as long as necessary to provide tier testing services. Inactive player data may be archived or removed after extended periods of inactivity.

Your Rights

You have the right to request that we remove your gameplay data from our system. To exercise this right, please contact us using the information below.
